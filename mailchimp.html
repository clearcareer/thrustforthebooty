DementiaHack will be held March 4-5. Hundreds will build solutions to tackle challenges in dementia, Alzheimer's, and other neuro-degenerative diseases. They win, we all win. DementiaHack is presented by Facebook, championed by the UK and Canadian Governments, hosted MaRS, and produced by HackerNest.
